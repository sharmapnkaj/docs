What is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of optimizing a website so that it ranks higher on search engines like Google. The main goal of SEO is to increase organic traffic by improving website visibility in search results.

When users search for information online, Google displays the most relevant pages. SEO helps websites become more relevant and trustworthy, increasing their chances of appearing at the top of search results.

Types of SEO

1. On-Page SEO

On-page SEO includes keyword optimization, title tags, meta descriptions, headings, image optimization and quality content creation.

2. Off-Page SEO

Off-page SEO focuses on building backlinks and improving website authority.

3. Technical SEO

Technical SEO includes website speed optimization, mobile responsiveness, sitemap creation and indexing improvements.

Benefits of SEO

  • Increases website traffic
  • Improves online visibility
  • Generates quality leads
  • Builds brand authority
  • Provides long-term results

How SEO Works

Search engines crawl websites, index content and rank pages according to relevance and quality. Websites with strong SEO practices have a better chance of ranking higher and attracting more visitors.

Conclusion

SEO is one of the most effective digital marketing strategies. By using proper SEO techniques, businesses can improve rankings, increase traffic and grow their online presence.
